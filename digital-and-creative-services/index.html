At the intersection of marketing and technology

Geeth LLC offers the best of both worlds—the flexibility and expertise of a boutique firm, backed by the powerful recruiting engine and network of Geeth LLC, the mid-size IT staffing firm in the U.S. We have a foundation in applications staffing and services with a specialized focus on digital, creative and interactive skill sets.
